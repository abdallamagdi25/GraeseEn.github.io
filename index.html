The Process: A Technology-Driven Solution

Our process is a sophisticated, two-train manufacturing enterprise. A re-refining stage purifies used oil, and a compounding stage engineers it into high-performance greases, all orchestrated by our "Sentient Plant" architecture.

🧪 Stage 1: Alchemy of Renewal – Re-refining Used Oil

We transform used oil into pristine base oil via a multi-step process: rigorous quality control of feedstock, pre-treatment (dewatering & de-fueling), core refining using superior Thin-Film Vacuum Distillation to prevent thermal degradation [17], and finishing with catalytic hydrotreating to produce clear, stable base oil meeting API Group I and II specifications. [1, 14]

⚙️ Stage 2: Formulation Engineering – Crafting Greases

Using our high-quality re-refined base oil, we compound a versatile portfolio of finished greases. The core process is a precisely controlled saponification reaction in a pressurized reactor [21], determining the final properties of the grease.

Lithium Complex: High-temp (>260°C), versatile for automotive & industrial use. [23]
Calcium Sulfonate: Superior water resistance (>300°C), ideal for marine & heavy-duty tasks.
Polyurea: Excellent thermal stability for sealed-for-life applications like EV motors. [22]

🛡️ Governance, Safety & Environmental Stewardship

Regulatory Compliance

Designed to meet or exceed all relevant standards, including EPA's Used Oil Management Standards (40 CFR Part 279), with strict protocols for storage, transport, and a prohibition on mixing with hazardous wastes. [8]

Process Safety Management

A multi-layered safety approach combining robust engineering controls, automated safety interlocks in the DCS/PLC, and detailed Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). [1]

Quantifiable Environmental Impact

Every gallon of re-refined oil displaces 42 gallons of virgin crude, uses ~67% less energy, and prevents soil and groundwater contamination from improper disposal. [9]
